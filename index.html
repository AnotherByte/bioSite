My name is Gloria Beatty. I was born and grew up in Menasha, WI. My mother and father had four children, two boys and two girls. Even as we grew up and moved out, we have all stayed close to home and get together quite often. I have been married to my wonderful husband for 41 years now, and we have one son.

I am a surface mount repair technician. I work at a company that assembles circuit boards for all kinds of products from soda fountains, to health care equipment, to military equipment. My job is to repair the boards that have failed inspection after either manual or automated assembly. Sometimes the issue is that a person misread the instructions and placed the wrong part on the boards or they put them in the wrong location. Usually when its an issue with the automated machines assembling the boards, the issues come from damaged parts either from being broken during assembly or they were damaged before we even got them. Any boards that I do repair, easily save the company hundreds of dollars each as the parts are not cheap and it takes a lot of work to set up the assembly lines to redo any boards.
